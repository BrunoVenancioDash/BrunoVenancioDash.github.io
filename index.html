About LoBlanc

Finance that regenerates

LoBlanc Investments is a CVM-registered asset manager specialized in structuring financial vehicles that connect decarbonization and environmental sustainability projects with institutional investors and foreign capital.

We operate at the intersection of financial markets and the low-carbon economy, providing the infrastructure required to turn environmental assets into real investment opportunities with governance, transparency, and returns.

Our Services

LoBlanc's 4 Pillars

PILLAR 01

Investment Fund Structuring

We create regulated financial vehicles, including private equity funds, ESG thematic funds, and carbon-credit structures, transforming environmental assets into tradable instruments with liquidity and governance.

Carbon Funds FIP Thematic ESG
PILLAR 02

Project Fundraising and Financing

We originate decarbonization projects with industries and structure access to green credit lines, international climate funds, and blended-finance mechanisms backed by foreign capital.

Green Credit Blended Finance International Capital
PILLAR 03

Credit Brokerage and Trading

A dedicated trading desk for buying and selling carbon credits in voluntary and regulated markets, connecting generators and buyers with transparency and legal certainty.

Trading OTC Regulated Market CBEs e CRVEs
PILLAR 04

Asset Valuation Advisory

We financially assess emissions inventories, price projects under international standards, and advise companies on certifying and monetizing their environmental assets.

Verra Gold Standard ESG Monetization
